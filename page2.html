Hospital bag list

Have you ever wondered what should you pack for your hospital stay after delivery? Well here is a list of essential both for mummy and baby.

Baby Must have

  • Long sleeved grows x6
  • Vests x6
  • Cardigan
  • Hat
  • Pre-washed baby towel
  • Pack of nappies (new born size)
  • Baby bibs x6
  • Baby blankets x2
  • Cot sheet x2
  • Car seat

Mother must have

  • Maternity notes with Birth plan
  • Personal Items (toiletries, lip balm)
  • Breasts pad
  • Sanitary Towel
  • Dressing gown/slippers/spare nightwear
  • Dark coloured towel
  • Light snacks
  • Mobile phone / camera
  • Relevant prescriptions
